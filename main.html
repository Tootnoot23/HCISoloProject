OUR SOLAR SYSTEM

Our solar system consists of the sun, and also everything that bound to it by gravity the planets Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. dwarf planets such as Pluto; dozens of moons; and millions of asteroids, comets, and meteoroids. Beyond our own solar system, we have discovered thousands of planetary systems orbiting other stars in the Milky Way.

SPACE FUN FACT:

There are more stars in the universe than grains of sands of earth.
